About Me




Hello, I am a senior studying classics and world language education at the University of Oklahoma.

In the classics domain I am currently interested in Bucolic Poetry, the study of early religious philosophy, and the speeches of great Roman orators like Cicero and Hortensius.

Outside of the classics I am very interested in religious philosophy from around the world, I have spent time studying Christianity, Judaism, Zoroastrianism and Buddhism.

I am involved in many organizations on campus here at OU. I am currently the President of the Mu Chapter of Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia, and the baritone section leader in the Pride of Oklahoma. I am currently a student member of the Oklahoma Foreign Language Teachers' Association and the American Classical League.

In my free time I enjoy cycling, reading, photography and good company.
